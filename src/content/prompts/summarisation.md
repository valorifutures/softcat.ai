title: "Dense Document Summariser"
description: "Extract the key points from a long document without losing important detail."
category: "summarisation"
tags: [summarisation, research, reading]
prompt: |
  Summarise the following document. I need the key points without reading the whole thing.

  Give me:

  **One-sentence summary** (what is this about and why does it matter?)

  **Key findings or claims** (bullet list, max 7 points, ordered by importance)

  **What it recommends or concludes** (what should I do or think differently based on this?)

  **What it does not cover** (gaps, caveats, or things it sidesteps)

  Be specific. Use numbers and names where the document gives them. Do not paraphrase vaguely.

  ---

  [paste document here]
draft: false
---

Works on papers, reports, meeting transcripts, long threads, and documentation. Gets the useful parts without the filler.

Paste anything text-based. Works well on PDFs if you extract the text first.
