The honeymoon period is over. Companies are hitting AI cost walls and scrambling for solutions while mobile deployment finally gets the tools it needs.

## Google pays SpaceX nearly a billion monthly for compute

Google is [paying SpaceX $920 million per month](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/05/google-will-pay-spacex-920m-per-month-for-compute/) for compute power. A Google rep blamed "unexpected demand" for recently launched AI products. That's either a massive miscalculation or AI usage is growing faster than anyone anticipated.

## Gemma 4 gets proper mobile optimisation

Google DeepMind released [Gemma 4 QAT checkpoints](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/06/05/google-deepmind-releases-gemma-4-qat-checkpoints-q4_0-and-a-new-mobile-format-cut-on-device-memory/) with Q4_0 quantisation and a new mobile format that cuts on-device memory. Finally, someone's taking edge deployment seriously instead of just shrinking cloud models. The mobile format is the interesting bit here.

## NVIDIA tackles inference startup times

NVIDIA released [Dynamo Snapshot](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/06/05/nvidia-ai-releases-dynamo-snapshot-a-criu-based-fast-startup-system-for-ai-inference-on-kubernetes/), a CRIU-based system for fast AI inference startup on Kubernetes. It checkpoints and restores vLLM workers instead of cold starting them. Smart move when every second of downtime costs real money.

## Perplexity builds hybrid inference routing

Perplexity announced a [hybrid local-server inference orchestrator](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/06/05/perplexity-ai-introduces-hybrid-local-server-inference-orchestrator-for-personal-computer-automatic-on-device-and-cloud-task-routing/) that automatically routes tasks between on-device and cloud models. This is the logical next step after everyone realised running everything in the cloud is expensive. Proper task routing could be a game changer for cost management.

## Token costs finally hit reality

TechCrunch reports on [AI's runaway costs](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/05/the-token-bill-comes-due-inside-the-industry-scramble-to-manage-ais-runaway-costs/), with one source noting the shift from "tokenmaxxing and go fast" to "we need guardrails, how do we control this?" The party's over and finance teams are asking awkward questions.
