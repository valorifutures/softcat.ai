# SOFT CAT .ai — Brand Style Sheet

## Name
- **Full**: SOFT CAT .ai
- **Acronym**: **S**mart **O**utputs **F**rom **T**rained **C**onversational **A**I **T**echnology
- Always written as **SOFT CAT** (all caps, two words). The `.ai` suffix in neon green when used digitally.

## Identity
- Maintained by **Valori**, an anonymous collective
- Always "we", never "I". No names, no faces, no credentials
- Tone: knowledgeable insiders, not thought leaders

## Visual Effects
- **Glassmorphism**: Semi-transparent gradient backgrounds with `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`
- **Card glow**: Section-coloured box-shadow on hover
- **Card lift**: Subtle `translateY(-2px)` on hover
- **Staggered fade-in**: Cards animate in sequence on page load
- **Ambient glow**: Large blurred orbs behind content grids
- **Banner feathering**: Images fade to void at edges

## Voice & Copy Rules
- Short sentences. Casual but not sloppy
- No em dashes. No semicolons (except code)
- Paragraphs: 2-3 sentences max
- Lead with the point
- **Banned words**: leveraging, utilising, comprehensive, robust, seamless, delve, tapestry, landscape, "in order to", "it's worth noting"
- Tags: lowercase, hyphenated, specific

## Logo Usage
- Terminal-style monospace aesthetic
- `SOFT CAT` in bright white with green glow
- `.ai` suffix in neon green
